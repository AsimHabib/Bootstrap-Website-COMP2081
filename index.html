Our Story

Automoblox is a brand name of an educational wooden car construction toy designed by Patrick Calello and produced by Automoblox Company, LLC of Roseland, New Jersey. The toy consists of wooden car body sections with patented plastic interconnects, polycarbonate wheels and rubber tyres, plastic passengers and polycarbonate screens.

Need Spare Parts?

Each car can be disassembled into its parts and re-assembled, parts from different cars can be combined to allow children to design their own models. The original range consisted of three models, the C9 Sports Coupe, the T9 Pickup Truck and the S9 Sedan. The range was extended in 2005 to include the X9 SUV, the A9 Compact and the M9 Sportsvan.
